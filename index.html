About the Show

Mr. Robot is a psychological thriller TV series created by Sam Esmail. The show follows the story of a cybersecurity engineer and hacker with dissociative identity disorder who is recruited by an insurrectionist insidious group known as "fsociety."

Main Characters

Elliot Alderson

The protagonist, a cybersecurity engineer and hacker with a troubled past.

Mr. Robot

The enigmatic figure who recruits Elliot into fsociety.
